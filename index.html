Thanks for visiting my site. My name is Max Rosenberg, and I'm a 23-year-old Staff Consultant at Crowe. I graduated from the University of Michigan with a major in Information Science. I'm from Manhattan Beach, California where some of my favorite hobbies are surfing and running. I created this site as part of a project for a web design class in school, and have continued to add and make edits to it as a side hobby. On this site you can find summaries of some of my work experiences and my most impactful academic projects. Additionally, I love reading books, listening to music, and watching movies, so you'll find my favorites from all three categories. Enjoy!
