Overview

There are three main projects which I felt I learned a significant amount from during my time at Michigan. During my sophomore year in Entrepreneurial Marketing, I created a lean marketing plan with one other student for a technology startup we came up with. The same year while working with 8 other students in the entrepreneurship subcommittee of the Michigan Business Group, we worked with a Ross MBA student on her startup and advised her on which business entity would be best for her unique organization. Finally, in the fall of my junior year, 2 other students and myself used newly-learned Python skills to analyze large quantities of data to demonstrate the effect of restaurant quality on tourism numbers in nations around the world (pre-COVID of course). Feel free to take a look at our deliverables on all these projects!
